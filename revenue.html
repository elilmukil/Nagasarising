Revenue Generation

We will introduce various proposals to come up with ways to raise public revenue.

  1. Generating huge savings by eliminating all financial loopholes (frauds, abuses, and wastes)

  2. Generating huge savings by stopping all kinds of rampant extortions, unabated taxations, and non-charitable donations

  3. Generating huge savings by turning bad debts into good debts

  4. Collecting Property Taxes in all urban areas:
    • All commercial buildings: 2% of its market value each year
    • All residential houses: 1% of its market value each year
    • All vacant lands: 1/2% of its market value each year

  5. Collecting Income Taxes:
    • All who are within the income bracket of Rs.15 to Rs. 25 lakhs must pay 5% of their total income, and those who make Rs. 25 lakhs or more must pay 8% of their income.   6. Collecting Corporate/Business Taxes: All business enterprises making Rs.50 lakhs or more must pay10% of its gross income. But this percentage point on taxes could be reduced depending of the number of jobs created for the local people.

  7. Collecting Sale Taxes: Liquor sale (55%), cigarettes sale (20%), lottery, entertainment, and on all others things except grocery items.

  8. Collecting Fees: Vehicle registration, driving licenses, use of public buildings, waste disposal services, water bill, electricity bill, affidavit stamps, etc.

  9. Collecting Fines: Public fund embezzlement (100%), destruction of public property, tax evasion, building code violation, forgery of documents, operating business without licenses, traffic violations, driving under the influence of alcohol, driving without license, possession of drugs, sale of liquor or tobacco to minors, employment of underage children, hiring of undocumented migrants, minor criminal offenses, carrying of fire-arms in public places, public drunkenness, littering in public places, etc.

  10. Generating Earnings through Financial Investments (e.g., FDI), public-private business ventures, etc.

  11. Generating Revenue from Mineral/Natural Resources: We will initially tap into the expertise of some foreign companies for oil & mineral exploration at minimum charge

  12. Fighting for the Maximum Amount of State-Shares and Central Grants: We will have some top-movers with well-equipped Office in Delhi to manage all fund-raising activities.
