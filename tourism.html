Green Tourism

The amount of global travel is expected to double in the next 20 years. Thus, tourism presents as one of the best revenue sources for any country that will open its borders for people to travel freely.

Only when tourists and business persons have the opportunity to spend their monies in Nagaland can the Nagas become self-sustaining and prosperous. But more importantly, tourism is the best avenue to show the best face of the Naga people to the world.

Mission-Statement: Acknowledging ourselves as stewards of God’s land, we will use Nagaland as a place of service to bless people of every color, caste or creed with an unforgettable taste of life in the hills and an exciting experience of natural beauty, God’s beautiful creation.

What the Government Must Do

Brand-Building: Make Nagaland the first “Green State” in India.

Tourists’ Access and Safety: Make the Inner Line Permit (ILP) easy to all visiting Indian nationals, lift the Restricted Area Permit (RAP) for all foreign tourists as long as they can produce valid visas, and ensure law and order throughout the State.

Transportation: Introduce our own plane services through a public-private partnership. Also, introduce helicopter and cable car services; build and maintain roads.

Tourist Sites: Build and operate a wildlife sanctuary; preserve and nationalize all historical sites, monuments, antique pieces, ancient records, native birds and plants.

What Private Enterprises Can Do

Saramati Mountain Retreat Center: Could be located in a spectacular mountain side of Saramati in Kiphire District where family vacations, church retreats, corporate meetings and family camps can be held. The buildings could be log cabins, having multiple bedrooms, large kitchen, coffee bar, dining hall, and a meeting hall … equipped with all modern amenities like TVs, ceiling-mounted projectors, Internet connection, etc.

Vacation Homes for Rent: Build cottages and homes that can be leased or rent out to vacationers, retired-people, and others who wanting to stay longer.

Hideout Jungle Homes: Build and rent vacationers’ homes—for them to enjoy complete privacy and solitude away from the hustle and bustle of city life, a place surrounded by scenic natural beauty and sounds of birds for an experience of an uncommon indulgence in natural therapies.

Lovers’ Resort: Build and rent a 4-star level resort where married couples can celebrate their honeymoons or wedding anniversaries; a special place enclosed within a secluded tropical style compound, complete with personal swimming pool and other modern amenities, including candle lit dinners, breakfasts, therapeutic messages, etc.

Treehouses: Build various sizes of treehouses with fully furnished rooms and modern amenities like those found in the State of Kerala.

Mountain Activities: Create services for mountain trekking and rock climbing, camping and hiking, animal-spotting and bird-watching.

Survival Training: Conduct survival psychology and team building, making fire without matches, reading the land, land navigation, campsite selection, shelter location and construction options, water location and purification, food procurement, nature observation skills, basic wilderness first aid, administering CPR, emergency signaling.

Corporate Team-Building Workshop: The places to be equipped with meeting rooms, teaching tools, plus plenty of spaces for indoor games and outdoor sports.

Customer Services: The Nagas must excel themselves in customer services. Most tourists pay for customer services more than anything else.

Water-Works: Create man-made waterfalls, beautiful ponds, and artfully designed bridges of various kinds.

Water Activities: Build man-made lakes in all districts for vacationers to enjoy everything from water activities (swimming, boating, and fishing) to just relaxing by the lakes or playing games on covered docks.

Art Displays: Display artfully designed stone sculptures, metal sculptures and wooden sculptures alongside footpaths, at rest-places, and in parks.

Signs and Directions: Place beautiful directional signs and engraved messages on stones alongside footpaths, at rest-places and in parks.

Amusement Parks and Recreational Centers: Children playgrounds, cultural exhibition, music programs, shooting and archery and other entertainment activities.

Retreat Centers: Youth camps, corporate workshops, business conferences.

Tourist Services: Provide tours and travel services; visas and passport services; currency-exchange and Internet services.

Transportations: Horse carriage rides, golf cart services, motorbike-on-rent, manual transportations, tour guides.

Be-a-Naga-for-a-Day Package: Sleeping in Naga heritage cottages, tasting Naga cuisine, experiencing Naga hospitality, and trying some Naga cultural dance.

Scout & Guide Programs: Make it possible for young people to develop the spirit of cooperation, learn the democratic values of community living, and be inculcated with a sense of civic duties.

Resulting Benefits to be Expected

  • Make Nagaland well-known both nationally and internationally

  • Create thousands of new jobs: park managers, maintenance workers, security polices, travel agents, tour guides, hotel managers, receptionists, waiters, chefs & cooks, store managers, shop keepers, event-managers, musicians, singers, cultural dancers, comedians, taxi drivers, plumbers, electricians, Internet operators, hair-dressers, etc.

  • Generate huge tourist-related revenue to the State Government, airline industry, hotel industry, taxi industry, entertainment industry, etc.

  • Collect a percentage of all tourism-related revenues as local tax for rural development.
